  • Methanosarcina mazei Goe1, Xanthomonas campestris pv. campestris ATCC 33913 and Xanthomonas axonopodis pv. citri 306 were added to GIB
GIB (Genome Information Broker for　Microbial Genomes) provides a comprehensive view of the complete microbial　genome sequences.Because the genome sequence data of Methanosarcina mazei Goe1 ,Xanthomonas campestris pv. campestris ATCC 33913 and Xanthomonas　axonopodis pv. citri 306 were released, we incorporated them to GIB, and　now you can search those data.
