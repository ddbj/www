S&W SEARCH, one of the homology search services provided through web and
e-mail server by DDBJ was terminated on April 7, 2006.
As was already notified in the preliminary notice issued on February 18,
2005, because of dissolution of the manufacturing company which had
provided the hardware for the S&W SEARCH service, the disrupt which have
been going on since March 22 is no longer recovered.
As the substitute service,
SSEARCH
which runs by similar algorithm with S&W SEARCH are also provided by DDBJ,
and this service is continuously available.
DDBJ appreciate very much for your access to S&W SEARCh service over
the past long years.
