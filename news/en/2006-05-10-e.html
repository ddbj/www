  • ARSA service emergency suspension
ARSA (All-round Retrieval Sequence and Annotation) is the DDBJ's new
retreival system, which is characteristic in the point of "high-speed"
and "XML based search system.
Because of the hardware system failures, ARSA is being suspended now.
Details are as follows.
  • Current condition: Query submission is impossible, in spite of
    viewing the ARSA page.
  • Substitute service: You can use
    getentry
    (search by accession numbers) and
    SRS
    (search by keywords) as the substitutive services.
  • Resume: It will be announced on the DDBJ HP as soon as after the
    system is recovered.
Thank you very much for your understanding and cooperation.
 
The service was resumed at 17:00 on May 10. We apologize for your inconvenience.
