  • Apology for the system failure of the Homology Search and ClustalW
DDBJ provides Homology Search Services and ClustalW WWW and WebAPI.

Because of the system failure, search and analysis had not been executed against the all queries during the following period. The details are as follows:
  • Period: from March 8, 2009 at 17:55 (JST) to March 9, 2009 at 09:08 (JST)
  • Services: FASTA, BLAST, PSI-BLAST, SSEARCH, HMMPFAM, ClustalW
If you had submitted queries during the above period, please submit them again. We apologize for your inconvenience.
