  • Data release of patent sequence from Korean Intellectual Property Office (KIPO)

DDBJ distributed the patent sequence data submitted from Korean Intellectual Property Office (KIPO).
KIPO data was issued the accession number as follows.
Nucleotide sequence data (122,339 entries): DI246931-DI369269
Amino acid sequence data (43,308 entries): DI661182-DI704489
They are available on getentry.
Please check KIPO data example at following URL.
Example of Nucleotide sequence data:
http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/getentry/na/DI246931
Example of Amino acid sequence data:
http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/getentry/aa/DI661182
Submission files were processed and submitted to DDBJ by Dr. Byungwook Lee at Korean Bioinformation Center (KOBIC) in collaboration with KIPO.
