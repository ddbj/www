  • 16S rRNA is newly added to DDBJ homology search databases
DDBJ provides homology search services by WWW and E-mail. "16S rRNA" is newly added to the searchable databases in FASTA, BLAST, and SSESRCH services. Deatils are as follows.
  • Database: 16S rRNA (Prokaryotes)
  • method:
    1. The 16S rRNA sequences which meets the following conditions were extracted from BCT division of the latest DDBJ periodical release.
    • include "rRNA" in Feature Key
    • include 16S", "RRS" or "SSU" in the product line of Qulifier Key.
  • After extraction, the sequences whose length is 300bp or less and which ar recognized as the antisense were removed.
  • Anonymous FTP:
    • File Name : 16S.seq.gz (FASTA format)
    • This data is also available from "Anonymous FTP of the DDBJ".
