The nucleotide sequence database collected and maintained by DDBJ is quarterly released online to the public and distributed in the magnetic media. We completed DDBJ Release 46 in Jul. 2001.
DDBJ Release 46 consists of 12,313,759 entries, and the number of bases reached 13,037,646,166.
