DDBJ released genome sequence data of a bacterium that degrades and assimilates polyethylene terephthalate (PET), Ideonella sakaiensis, which had been submitted by Kyoto University.

Reference
Yoshida S et al. (2016). Science 351, 1196-1199. DOI: 10.1126/science.aad6359

The accession numbers are as follows. They are available on getentry

WGS: BBYR01000001-BBYR01000227 (BBYR.gz) (227 entries)
