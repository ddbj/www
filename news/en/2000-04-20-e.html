  • Temporary stop and upgrade of SAKURA service
SAKURA (DDBJ DNA Data submission system) service will be stopped during the following period because of the upgrade of SAKURA.
Apr.29 (Sat) - May 7 (Sun)
The upgraded service of SAKURA will start after the period.
The new SAKURA system accepts almost all features/qualifiers that DDBJ/EMBL/GenBank provide. In addition it checks input data more strictly, which makes you find an error in your input data more easily.
As a result of many changes in the new system, the data format of the new system is not compatible with that of the current system.
If you are planning to do sequential submission, please complete it before the current system is stopped, or start the registration after the new SAKURA is released.
We appreciate your cooperation.
