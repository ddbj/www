DDBJ, EMBL-Bank/EBI, GenBank/NCBI hold the international collaborators meetings in every May for the porpose of the operation and the implement of the collaborative construction of the international nucleotide sequence database.
In the meeting, the three banks report each bank activities in the past year, and discussed some practical matters to maintain and to develop the nucleotide sequence database
under INSDC; International Nucleotide Sequence Database collaboration.
The report for The 19th International Collaborators Meeting was placed on the DDBJ HP. This report will also be provided in the DDBJ Mail Magazine No.28.
