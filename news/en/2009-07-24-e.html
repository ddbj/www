  • Release of new tobacco (Nicotiana tabacum) EST 65,102 entries
DDBJ newly released 65,102 entries of EST data derived from tobacco (Nicotiana tabacum), which had been submitted by Japan Tobacco Inc.

The accession numbers  (Anonymous FTP)  are as follows;

  • FS373218-FS438319 (Nicotiana_tabacum_EST_090723_1.seq.gz) (65,102 entries)

These entries were released as DDBJ daily updates on Jul. 23, 2009.
