  • Release of genome and transcriptome sequence data of Taiwan habu (Protobothrops mucrosquamatu)

DDBJ released genome and transcriptome sequence data of Taiwan habu (Protobothrops mucrosquamatu) which had been submitted by Okinawa Institute of Science and Technology Graduate University.

The accession numbers are as follows. They are available on getentry or DRASearch.

Genome
WGS: BCNE010000001-BCNE011421934 (BCNE.gz) (1,421,934 entries)
DRA: DRR049668, DRR049669 (DRA004189)
Transcriptome
TSA: IAAC01000001-IAAC01112307 (IAAC.gz) (112,307 entries)
DRA: DRR049635-DRR049665 (DRA004186)
