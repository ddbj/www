  • DAD Release Note

* Termination of providing CDS fasta files:
We formerly provided fasta files for nucleotide sequences corresponding to CDS features in the 'cdsdb' directory under the 'ddbj' directory;
ftp.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/ddbj_database/ddbj/cdsdb/

However, the files have not been used so frequently and we have provided an alternative service via our web APIs, WABI;
https://www.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/services/wabi-e.html

So, we decided to terminate the generation of CDS fasta files.
See also the following URL to use WABI;
https://www.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/services/getentry-e.html#ge_program
