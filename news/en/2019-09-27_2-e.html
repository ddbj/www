  • Release of sequence data of a species of tropical reef corals, (Acropora solitaryensis) and its algal symbionts

DDBJ released sequence data of a species of tropical reef corals, (Acropora solitaryensis) and its algal symbionts (Cladocopium goreaui and Durusdinium trenchii), which had been submitted by University of Tsukuba.

The accession numbers are as follows.
They are available on getentry or DRASearch.

TSA
  • Acropora solitaryensis ICPH01000001-ICPH01040036 (40,036 entries) ICPH.gz
  • Cladocopium goreaui ICPI01000001-ICPI01003338 (3,338 entries) ICPI.gz
  • Durusdinium trenchii ICPJ01000001-ICPJ01004504 (4,504 entries) ICPJ.gz
DRA
  • DRR169398-DRR169409 (DRA008078)
