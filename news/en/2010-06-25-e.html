Termination of DDBJ-XML output format

DDBJ-XML is one of the data output formats, which DDBJ designed based on XML. DDBJ discontinues providing DDBJ-XML data on Jun. 25, 2010. The details are as follows. We apologize for your inconvenience. Thank you for your understanding and cooperation. Termination schedule: Jun. 25, 2010
