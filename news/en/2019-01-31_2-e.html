  • MiGAP, Microbial Genome Annotation Pipeline, will be closed

Dear MiGAP users,

MiGAP, Microbial Genome Annotation Pipeline, will be closed February 2019.

  • Applications for MiGAP using and password change will be closed at 17:00, 31 January (Thursday).
  • Login to MiGAP will be impossible after 15 February, so, please download the necessary analyzed data.
  • Your data will be deleted after the end of service.
  • We introduce DFAST as alternative analysis service, a successor of MiGAP.
    User accounts and data are not taken over.
    Please use DFAST from the following site.
    https://dfast.nig.ac.jp/
