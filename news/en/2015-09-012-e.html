  • Release of genome sequence data of Lotus japonicus

DDBJ released genome sequence data of Lotus japonicus which had been submitted by Kazusa DNA Research Institute.

Reference
Database: miyakogusa.jp

The accession numbers are as follows. They are available on getentry or DRASearch.

WGS: BABK02000001-BABK02044464 (BABK.gz) (44,464 entries)
CON: DG000091-DG000096 (6 entries)
DRA: DRA001236 (DRR014723-DRR014732)
