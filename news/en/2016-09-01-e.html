DDBJ released genome, GSS, RAD-Seq, RNA-Seq and EST data of Japanese morning glory (Ipomoea nil) which had been submitted by National Institute for Basic Biology.

The accession numbers are as follows. They are available on getentry or DRASearch.

Genome
WGS: BDFN01000001-BDFN01003416 (3,416 entries) BDFN.gz
mitochondrion: AP017303
chloroplast: AP017304
DRA: DRR013917-DRR013926 (DRA001121), DRR024668 (DRA002710), DRR048755-DRR048757 (DRA004158)
GSS
GSS: GA933005-GA974698 (41,694 entries)
RAD-Seq
DRA: DRR026831-DRR027252 (DRA002758)
RNA-Seq
DRA: DRR024544-DRR024549 (DRA002647)
EST
EST: HY917605-HY949060 (31,456 entries)
