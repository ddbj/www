  • Release of genome sequence data of a coral symbiont, Symbiodinium minutum

DDBJ released genome sequence data of a coral symbiont, Symbiodinium minutum, which had been submitted by Okinawa Institute of Science and Technology.

Reference; A coral symbiont genome decoded for first time
The accession numbers are as follows. They are available on getentry and DRASearch.

    scaffold CON: DF239013-DF260911 (21,899 entries)
    WGS: BASF01000001-BASF01033816 (BASF.gz) (33,816 entries)
    raw data: DRR003834-DRR003864

    GSS: GA393224-GA605429 (212,206 entries)
