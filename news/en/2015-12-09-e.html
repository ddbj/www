DDBJ released genome sequence data of azuki bean (Vigna angularis var. angularis cv. Shumari) which had been submitted by National Institute of Agrobiological Sciences.

The accession numbers are as follows. They are available on getentry or DRASearch.

AP015045-AP017294 (2,250 entries)

DRR031705 (DRA003236)

DRR032984-DRR033067 (DRA003467)
