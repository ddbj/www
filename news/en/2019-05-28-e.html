The 1000 Genomes datasets copied from NCBI ftp are available at DDBJ ftp.

1000 Genomes datasets are available at the following sites.

  • DDBJ ftp: ftp://ftp.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/mirror_database/1000genomes/
  • NCBI ftp: ftp://ftp-trace.ncbi.nih.gov/1000genomes/ftp/
  • EBI ftp: ftp://ftp.1000genomes.ebi.ac.uk/vol1/ftp/
  • AWS: https://registry.opendata.aws/1000-genomes/
