  • Release of genome sequence data of Theileria orientalis strain Shintoku
DDBJ newly released genome sequence data derived from Theileria orientalis strain Shintoku, which had been submitted by Hokkaido University.(Available by getentry )

The accession numbers are as follows;

  • chromosomes : AP011946-AP011949 (4 entries)
  • apicoplast : AP011950
  • mitochondrion : AP011951
