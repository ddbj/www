  • (Dec. 28 - Jan. 5) Suspension of BI-DDBJ activity during the New Year Holidays

Bioinformation and DDBJ Center will be closed for the New Year Holidays from December 28, 2019 (Sat) to January 5, 2020 (Sun).
Please note that DDBJ Nucleotide Sequence Submission System (NSSS) will be unavailable. Schedule is as follows.

BI-DDBJ office close: December 28, 2019 (Sat) - January 5, 2020 (Sun)
NSSS: December 26, 2019 (Thu) 18:00 - January 6, 2020 (Mon) 10:00 (JST)

NIG Supercomputer and DDBJ Web services (except NSSS) are available during the New Year Holidays. After January 6 (JST), we will respond to requests or questions which are received during this period.

Thank you in advance for your understanding and cooperation.
