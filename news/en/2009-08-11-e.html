  • Redistribution of genomic sequence (build 4) of the cultivar Nipponbare of Japanese rice (Oryza sativa Japonica Group) assigned with RAP annotation
The entries corresponding to complete sequences of japonica rice chromosomes
were updated from build 3 to build 4.
Their accession numbers are AP008207-AP008218.
The updated contents were the results annotated by The Second Rice Annotation
Project Meeting (RAP2) which was an international rice genome annotation project
organized by Japanese research groups.
By this updating, AP008207-AP008218 entries were redistributed with
approximately 28,000 CDS (protein coding region) features.

The accession numbers  (Anonymous FTP)  are as follows ;

  • AP008207-AP008218 (Oryza_sativa_Japonica_Group_CON_090807_1.seq.gz) (12 entries)
