DDBJ released genome sequence data of eggplant (Solanum melongena), which had been submitted by Kazusa DNA Research Institute.

Reference;
Eggplant Genome DataBase

The accession numbers are as follows. They are available on getentry or DRASearch.

    WGS: BAUE01000001-BAUE01143048 (BAUE.gz) (143,048 entries)
    SRA: DRR014074-DRR014076
