DDBJ Read Archive (DRA) collects and distributes raw output data from next-generation sequencers with NCBI Sequence Read Archive and EBI Sequence Read Archive.You can download the metadata XML and fastq files released from DRA/ERA/SRA at DRA Download (FASTQ) site.
