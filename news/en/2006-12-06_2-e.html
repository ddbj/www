  • Codon usage table is available at GIB
GIB (Genome Information Broker) provides an integrated search of Bacteria, Archaea, Eukaryota complete genome sequences.
The codon usage table was added to GIB. The frequency of the codon use in the chromosomes and in the plasmids is available.
Click on "Codon Usage" from the left menu of the page of each species.
