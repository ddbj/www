(Change) The termination of SAKURA has been changed on October 31, 2012 at 17:00(JST).

DDBJ will change 'SAKURA' into a new submission system 'DDBJ Nucleotide Sequence Submission System' at the end of September, 2012.
After changing a system, you cannot use SAKURA. Please check the following notes.
Notes about 'SAKURA'
  • Data of saved entries and templates in progress are not carried on into the new submission system.
    So please complete your registration prior to the termination of SAKURA.

DDBJ has accepted nucleotide sequence data submissions by 'SAKURA' from 1995. Thank you for using SAKURA for a long time.
If you have any question on SAKURA, please send your message from Contact us (SAKURA Support).
Thank you for your understanding and cooperation.
