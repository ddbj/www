  • Release of genome data of wine grapes (Vitis vinifera 4 cultivars)

DDBJ released genome data (WGS) of wine grapes (Vitis vinifera 4 cultivars) which had been submitted by Agricultural University of Georgia.

The accession numbers are as follows. They are available on getentry.

cultivar accession number file
Chkhaveri BDSO01000001-BDSO01000021 (21 entries) BDSO.gz
Saperavi BDSQ01000001-BDSQ01000021 (21 entries) BDSQ.gz
Meskhetian green BDSR01000001-BDSR01000021 (21 entries) BDSR.gz
Rkatsiteli BDSS01000001-BDSS01000021 (21 entries) BDSS.gz
