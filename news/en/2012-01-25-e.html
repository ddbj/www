  • Release of genome sequence data of eucaly (Eucalyptus camaldulensis)
DDBJ released genome sequence data derived from eucaly (Eucalyptus camaldulensis), which had been submitted by Kazusa DNA Research Institute.
Reference : Eucalyptus camaldulensis Genome Database
The accession numbers are as follows ;
  • CON          DF097775 - DF126446 ( 28,672 entries)  (Available by getentry)
  • WGS         BADO01000001 - BADO01274001 (BADO.gz) ( 274,001 entries)
  • raw data     DRA000466, DRA000467
