  • Release of new fission yeast GSS 113,551 entries and EST 101,079 entries
DDBJ newly released GSS: 113,551 entries and EST: 101,079 entries derived from fission yeast (Schizosaccharomyces pombe ), which had been submitted by Osaka City University.The accession numbers  (Anonymous FTP)  are as follows ;
  • GSS: FT321169-FT434719 (Schizosaccharomyces_pombe_GSS_100728_1.seq.gz) (113,551 entries)
  • EST: FY072959-FY174037 (Schizosaccharomyces_pombe_EST_100728_1.seq.gz) (101,079 entries)
These entries were released as DDBJ daily updates on Jul. 28, 2010.(Photo: Integrated Database Project)
