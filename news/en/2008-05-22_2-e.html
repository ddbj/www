  • Removal of some databases from ARSA

ARSA ( All-round Retrieval of Sequence and Annotation ) is the high-speed retrieval system , which is provided by DDBJ via WWW.<dd>Currently, ARSA provides 24 searchable databases including the following 6 PFAM related databases.

<ul><br>
    <li> PFAMA<br></li>
    <li>PFAMB<br></li>
    <li>PFAMHMMLS<br></li>
    <li>PFAMHMMFS<br></li>
    <li>SWISSPFAM<br></li>
    <li>PFAMSEED<br></li>
</ul>
Among them, 4 databases ( PFAMHMMLS, PFAMHMMFS, SWISSPFAM, PFAMSEED )
will be removed from ARSA because of redundant subset of PFAMA as of May 28, 2008.
PFAMA and PFAMB are continuously available.
Thank you very much for your understanding and cooperation.
The removal work was finished. ( May. 28, 2008 at 11:45 )

</dd>
