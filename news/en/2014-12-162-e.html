DDBJ released genome and transcriptome sequence data of hops (Humulus lupulus), 2 varieties, which had been submitted by Suntory Global Innovation Center Limited.

The accession numbers are as follows. They are available on getentry or DRASearch.

Humulus lupulus var. cordifolius
    scaffold CON: LD000001-LD132476 (132,476 entries)
    WGS: BBPB01000001-BBPB01292698 (BBPB.gz) (292,698 entries)
    DRA run: DRR024452-DRR024456

Humulus lupulus var. lupulus
    scaffold CON: LD132477-LD264952 (132,476 entries)
    WGS: BBPC01000001-BBPC01292698 (BBPC.gz) (292,698 entries)
    DRA run: DRR024392-DRR024447

Humulus lupulus var. lupulus
    TSA: LA328023-LA715951 (387,929 entries)
    DRA run: DRR024457-DRR024463
