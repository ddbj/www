DDBJ released gene expression data of domestic silkworm (Bombyx mori), which had been submitted by National Agriculture and Food Research Organization.

The accession numbers are as follows.
They are available on getentry or DRASearch.

TSA
  • ICPK01000001-ICPK01051926 (51,926 entries) ICPK.gz
DRA
  • DRR186474-DRR186503 (DRA008737)
GEA
  • E-GEAD-315 ftp


Related data which are not the DDBJ Center databases' scope are deposited to DBarchive of National Bioscience Database Center (NBDC) (doi:10.18908/lsdba.nbdc02443-000.V001)

Publication using these data were published from bioRxiv (doi:10.1101/805978)
