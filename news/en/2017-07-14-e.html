  • JBI (Japan alliance for Bioscience Information) portal site is now open.

DDBJ has launched the JBI (Japan alliance for Bioscience Information) portal site in collaboration with National Bioscience Database Center (NBDC)/JST, Database Center for Life Science (DBCLS)/Joint Support-Center for Data Science Research/ROIS, and Protein Data Bank Japan(PDBj)/Institute for Protein Research/Osaka University. This portal site provides one-stop service of databases and tools which are helpful in various scenes of the life science research.
