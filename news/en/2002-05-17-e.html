  • Streptomyces coelicolor A3(2) and Thermoanaerobacter tengcongensis MB4T were added to GIB
GIB (Genome Information Broker for　Microbial Genomes) provides a comprehensive view of the complete microbial　genome sequences.　Because the genome sequence data of Streptomyces coelicolor A3(2) and　Thermoanaerobacter tengcongensis MB4T were released,　we incorporated them to GIB, and now you can search those data.
Streptomyces coelicolor A3(2)
Institute: The Sanger Centre
Reference: Complete genome sequence of the model actinomycete　Streptomyces coelicolor A3(2), Nature 417, 141-147(2002)
Thermoanaerobacter tengcongensis MB4T
Institute: Institute of Genetics and Development, Chinese Academy　of Sciences
Reference: A Complete Sequence of T. tengcongensis Genome, Genome　Res. 12 (5), 689-700 (2002)
