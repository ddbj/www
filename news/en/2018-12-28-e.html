  • (December 28-January 9) Stop updating DRA Search

Updating of DRA Search are stopped due to maintenance.
The schedule is as follows.

December 28 (Fri), 2019 - January 9 (Wed), 2019

DRA Search will be also unavailable from January 9 (Wed), 2019 15:00(JST)

The DRA data released during the period will be available on DRA Search by degrees after the maintenance that is scheduled until January 17 (Thu), 2019.

FTP service is available as usual and the data are also mirrored to NCBI SRA and EBI SRA(ENA).
Please use NCBI SRA and EBI SRA(ENA) search services if necessary.

Thank you for your understanding and cooperation.
