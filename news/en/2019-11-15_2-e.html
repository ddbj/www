The Annotathon 2019 (Joint Research Program, ROIS-DS-JOINT) was held at the Database Center for Life Science (DBCLS) Kashiwanoha-campus in 13-14th, November, 2019.

From Bioinformation and DDBJ Center, senior curators Fukuda and Kodama joined and Fukuda gave presentation about the BioSample database. We discussed with BioSample submitters and users to improve our services.
