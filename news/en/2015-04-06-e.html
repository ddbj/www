DDBJ released transcriptome sequence data of common iceplant (Mesembryanthemum crystallinum), which had been submitted by Nagoya University.

The accession numbers are as follows. They are available on getentry or DRASearch.

TSA: FX891461-FX944976 (53,516 entries)
DRA Run: DRR018522-DRR018525
