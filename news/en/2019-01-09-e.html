  • Release of genome sequence data of 4 species of sea snakes

DDBJ released genome sequence data of 4 species of sea snakes, turtlehead sea snake (Emydocephalus ijimae), yellow-lipped sea krait (Laticauda colubrina), slender-necked sea snake (Hydrophis melanocephalus) and blue-ringed sea krait (Laticauda laticaudata), which had been submitted by Kyoto University.

The accession numbers are as follows.
They are available on getentry or DRASearch.

(December 28-January 9) Stop updating DRA Search
(January 8-17) DDBJ services will be unavailable

Laticauda colubrina

Hydrophis melanocephalus
