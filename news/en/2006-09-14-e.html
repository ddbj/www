  • Apology for the Defect of the result display in WWW getentry
getentry is the entry retrieval system by accession numbers etc,
which is provided by DDBJ via WWW
and E-mail servers.
Duinrg the following period, there were some search results in which a part of the
entry was not displayed in the screen.
  • Period: September 5, 2006 17:00 - September 6, 2006 16:00 (JST)
If you did the search using getentry during the above period, please do it again.
The defect had already been fixed, and now, the service works normally.
E-mail version getentry was not affected.
We apologize for your inconvenience.
