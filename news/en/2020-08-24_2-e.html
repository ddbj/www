  • Release of sequence data of ten species of birds and mammals

DDBJ released WGS sequence data of ten species of birds and mammals, which had been submitted by National Institute for Environmental Studies.

The accession numbers are as follows.
They are available on getentry.

whooper swan (Cygnus cygnus)
BMBB01000001-BMBB01025888 (25,888 entries) BMBB.gz

Japanese wood pigeon (Columba janthina nitens)
BMBC01000001-BMBC01015508 (15,508 entries) BMBC.gz

jungle crow (Corvus macrorhynchos)
BMBD01000001-BMBD01016523 (16,523 entries) BMBD.gz

tufted puffin (Fratercula cirrhata)
BMBE01000001-BMBE01033379 (33,379 entries) BMBE.gz

Steller's sea-eagle (Haliaeetus pelagicus)
BMBF01000001-BMBF01014266 (14,266 entries) BMBF.gz

Bali myna (Leucopsar rothschildi)
BMBG01000001-BMBG01013134 (13,134 entries) BMBG.gz

short-tailed albatross (Phoebastria albatrus)
BMBH01000001-BMBH01023454 (23,454 entries) BMBH.gz

Bonin flying fox (Pteropus pselaphon)
BMBI01000001-BMBI01007513 (7,513 entries) BMBI.gz

common murre (Uria aalge inornata)
BMBJ01000001-BMBJ01042406 (42,406 entries) BMBJ.gz

Japanese black bear (Ursus thibetanus japonicus)
BMBK01000001-BMBK01035282 (35,282 entries) BMBK.gz
