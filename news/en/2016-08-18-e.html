DDBJ released genome sequence data of Okinawa mozuku, Cladosiphon okamuranus, which had been submitted by Okinawa Institute of Science and Technology Graduate University.

  • Okinawa Mozuku - The Treasure Under the Sea

WGS: BDDF01000001-BDDF01004525 (4,525 entries) BDDF.gz
DRA: DRR059718-DRR059726 (DRA004654)
