DDBJ released genome sequence data of zoysiagrasses, 3 species which had been submitted by Kazusa DNA Research Institute.

The accession numbers are as follows. They are available on getentry or DRASearch.

Zoysia pacifica cv. Zanpa BCLH01000001-BCLH01011428 (BCLH.gz) (11,428 entries) DRR047288, DRR047290 (DRA004073)
