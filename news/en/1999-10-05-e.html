  • Elimination of EMBL, EMBLNEW and GBNEW from supernig and VPP500
EMBL, EMBLNEW and GBNEW databases were dropped from NIG mainframe computers(supernig and VPP500) as of October 1st, 1999.Now you can use only DDBJ and DDBJNEW on supernig and VPP500.It should be noted that the DDBJ/EMBL/GenBank International Nucleotide Sequence Database shares the same data.Difference among DDBJ, EMBL and GenBank databases are only their formats.
