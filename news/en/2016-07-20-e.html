DDBJ released genome sequence data of sub clover, Trifolium subterraneum cv. Daliak, which had been submitted by Kazusa DNA Research Institute.

Reference

  • Clover GARDEN

The accession numbers are as follows. They are available on getentry and DRASearch.

CON: DF973112-DF976994 (3,883 entries)
WGS: BCLP01000001-BCLP01066167 (BCLP.gz) (66,167 entries)
DRA: DRR018263-DRR018264 (DRA002213), DRR032035-DRR032043 (DRA003274)
