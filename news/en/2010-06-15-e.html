  • Release of new mouse (Mus musculus ) embryo small RNAs, MGA 397,593 entries
DDBJ newly released small RNA sequences, 397,593 entries of MGA data derived from mouse (Mus musculus) embryos, which had been submitted by National Institute of Neuroscience.The accession numbers are as follows:
  • ALAAA0000001-ALAAA0130942 (130,942 entries)
  • ALAAB0000001-ALAAB0116883 (116,883 entries)
  • ALAAC0000001-ALAAC0092019 (92,019 entries)
  • ALAAD0000001-ALAAD0057749 (57,749 entries)
Anonymous FTP: AL_resource_index.html These entries were released as DDBJ daily updates on Jun. 15, 2010.
