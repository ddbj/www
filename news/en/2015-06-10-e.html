DDBJ released genome and transcriptome sequence data of radish (Raphanus sativus cv. Aokubi) which had been submitted by Tokyo University of Agriculture.

Reference;
Database: NODAI Radish genome database

The accession numbers are as follows. They are available on getentry or DRASearch.

Genome sequence

scaffold CON: DF196826-DF236948 (40,123 entries)
WGS: BAOO01000001-BAOO01072909 (BAOO.gz) (72,909 entries)

DRA: DRA001016

Transcriptome sequence

DRA: DRA001003
