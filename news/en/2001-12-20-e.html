CLUSTALW is the program for multiple alignment and tree-making. DDBJ released an extended version of CLUSTALW on Dec. 7, 2001. The extended options are as follows:
- When you specify ALIGN "on" for multiple alignments, dot option is available. In this description, identical part of the sequences are indicated as dot (see example).
- When you specify TREE "on" to reconstruct a phylogenetic tree from nucleotide sequences, as a method for estimation of a genetic distance, Tamura, Tajima-Nei, Gojobori-Ishii-Nei 6-parameter and Tamura-Nei as well as Jukes-Cantor and Kimura 2-parameter, can be selected.
