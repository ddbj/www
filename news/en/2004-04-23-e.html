  • Silkworm Genome in WGS data released
DDBJ (DNA Data Bank of Japan) released silkworm (Bombyx mori) genomic nucleotide sequence data on Apr. 13, 2004. This data was submitted as WGS (Whole Genome Shotgun) data, and is the first WGS data that DDBJ released.
Please refer to this page for more information about WGS.
This data was submitted by Silkworm Genome Research Program, National Institute of Agrobiological Siences. Accession numbers released from DDBJ are BAAB01000001-BAAB01213289 (213,289 entries 381,906,524 bp).It corresponds to 80% of silkworm genome.
You can retrieve above entries via DDBJ getentry system
Reference
The Genome Sequence of Silkworm, Bombyx mori. DNA Res. 11, 27-35 (2004)
