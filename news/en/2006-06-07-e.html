  • Genome data was added to GIB
GIB (Genome Information Broker)
provides an integrated search of Bacteria, Archaea, Eukaryota complete
genome sequences.
Because the genome sequence data of Burkholderia cenocepacia AU 1054
was released by Joint Genome Institute, we incorporated it to GIB,
and now you can search those data.
