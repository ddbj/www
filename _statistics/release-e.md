CON and TPA divisions are not included in the release statistics due to
the following reasons. CON: CON division records are constructed from
other sequence records and their inclusion would be a form of
double-counting. TPA: TPA division records are not original data
obtained by submitters.

Because very large-scale sequence data has been distributed by
GenBank/ENA and DDBJ is not able to accomodate records with accession
numbers in a new format, our latest release lacks large portion of
GenBank/ENA's bulk sequence data.
