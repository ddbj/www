<ul>
    <li><i>Gramella forsetii KT0803 </i> (by Max Planck Institute)</li>
    <li><i>Aeromonas hydrophila subsp. hydrophila ATCC 7966 </i> (by The Institute for Genomic Research)</li>
</ul>
