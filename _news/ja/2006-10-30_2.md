    <li><i>Solibacter usitatus Ellin6076</i> (by Joint Genome Institute)</li>
    <li><i>Arthrobacter sp. FB24</i> (by Joint Genome Institute)</li>
    <li><i>Burkholderia cenocepacia HI2424</i> (by Joint Genome Institute)</li>
