    <li><i>Azoarcus sp. BH72</i> (by University of Bremen)</li>

    <li><i>Yersinia enterocolitica subsp. enterocolitica 8081</i> (by Sanger Institute)</li>
    <li><i>Mycobacterium sp. KMS</i> (by Joint Genome Institute)</li>
    <li><i>Pyrobaculum islandicum DSM 4184</i> (by Joint Genome Institute)</li>
    <li><i>Shewanella amazonensis SB2B</i> (by Joint Genome Institute)</li>
    <li><i>Psychromonas ingrahamii 37</i> (by Joint Genome Institute)</li>
