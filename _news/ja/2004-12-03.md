KSM-K16, J. Ferment. Bioeng. 78, 105-108 (1994)<br>- Purification and properties of an alkaline protease from alkalophilic Bacillus sp. KSM-K16, Appl. Microbiol. Biotechnol. 43, 473-481 (1995)<br>- The complete genome sequence of the alkaliphilic Bacillus clausii KSM-K16, Unpublished (2003)</small></html>
