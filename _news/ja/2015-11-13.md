<p><strong><a href="/ddbj/tsa.html">TSA</a></strong>: IAAA01000001-IAAA01132843 (132,843 entries) <a href="https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/tsa/IAAA.gz">IAAA.gz</a> <br><strong>DRA Run</strong>: DRR047015-DRR047017 </p>
