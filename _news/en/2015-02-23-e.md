<p>DDBJ released genome sequence data of Antarctic minke whale (<em>Balaenoptera bonaerensis</em>), which had been submitted by Kyoto University. </p>

<p><strong>The accession numbers</strong> are as follows. They are available on <a href="http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html" target="_blank">getentry</a> or <a href="http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/" target="_blank">DRASearch</a>. </p>

<p><strong><a href="/ddbj/con-e.html">scaffold CON</a></strong>: DF397027-DF818470 (421,444 entries) <br><strong><a href="/ddbj/wgs-e.html">WGS</a></strong>: BAUQ01000001-BAUQ01720900 (BAUQ.gz) (720,900 entries) <br><strong>DRA run</strong>: DRR014695 </p>
