<p>Dear MiGAP users, </p>

<p>MiGAP, Microbial Genome Annotation Pipeline, will be closed February 2019. </p>

<ul>
    <li>Applications for MiGAP using and password change will be closed at 17:00, 31 January (Thursday).</li>
    <li>Login to MiGAP will be impossible after 15 February, so, please download the necessary analyzed data.</li>
    <li>Your data will be deleted after the end of service. </li>
    <li>We introduce DFAST as alternative analysis service, a successor of MiGAP. <br>User accounts and data are not taken over. <br>Please use DFAST from the following site.<br><a href="https://dfast.nig.ac.jp/">https://dfast.nig.ac.jp/</a></li>
</ul>
