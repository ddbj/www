<p>Bioinformation and DDBJ Center will be closed for the New Year Holidays from December 29, 2021 (Wed) to January 3, 2022 (Mon).<br>Please note that <a href="/ddbj/websub-e.html">DDBJ Nucleotide Sequence Submission System (NSSS)</a> will be unavailable during 24/Dec/2021 18:00 - 4/Jan/2022 10:00. Schedule is as follows.</p><p>BI-DDBJ office close: December 29, 2021 (Wed) - January 3, 2022 (Mon)<br>NSSS: December 24, 2021 (Fri) 18:00 - January 4, 2022 (Tue) 10:00 (JST)</p><p>NIG Supercomputer and DDBJ Web services (except <a href="/ddbj/websub-e.html">NSSS</a>) are available during the New Year Holidays. After January 4 (JST), we will respond to requests or questions which are received during this period.</p>
<p>BioProject, BioSample, DRA, GEA, JGA and MetaboBank system is available during the period, however, reviewing and responding to any inquiry will stop from December 28 to January 5.</p>
