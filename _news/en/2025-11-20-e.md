The following DDBJ services will be unavailable due to emergency hardware maintenance.    
DRA validation, GEA validation will be suspended from 9:00(JST) 25th November 2025 (Tuesday).  
DDBJSearch, MetaboBank will be suspended from 17:00(JST) 25th November 2025 (Tuesday).   
DDBJ Account services will be suspended from 13:00-15:00(JST) on 25th November 2025 (Tuesday),
and from 14:00-17:00(JST) 26th November 2025 (Wednesday).  
During the suspension of DDBJ Account service, the SFTP login service for JGA/AGD and D-way will also be unavailable.  
We apologize for the short notice.
