## Release of sequence data of a hemiparasitic plant (Phtheirospermum japonicum)

DDBJ released sequence data of a hemiparasitic plant (<span
class="italic">Phtheirospermum japonicum</span>), which had been
submitted by Nara Institute of Science and Technology.

[The accession numbers](/documents/accessions.html) are as follows.  
They are available on
[getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or
[DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/).

[WGS](/ddbj/wgs-e.html)  
: BMAC01000001-BMAC01010559 (10,559 entries)
[BMAC.gz](ftp://ftp.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/ddbj_database/wgs/BM/BMAC.gz)

[DRA](/dra/index-e.html)  
: DRR033688-DRR033700 (DRA003500), DRR234111-DRR234135 (DRA010352)
