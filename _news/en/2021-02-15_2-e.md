DDBJ released sequence data of kuruma prawn (*Penaeus japonicus*), which had been submitted by Tokyo University of Marine Science and Technology.

[The accession numbers](/documents/accessions-e.html) are as follows.

They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/). 

[WGS](/ddbj/wgs-e.html)
: BOPN01000001-BOPN01018210 (18,210 entries) [BOPN.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BO/BOPN.gz) 

[DRA](/dra/index-e.html)
: DRR271272-DRR271287 (DRA011460)
