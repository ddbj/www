DDBJ released sequence data of Ayu sweetfish (*Plecoglossus altivelis*), which had been submitted by Tokyo University of Marine Science and Technology.

[The accession numbers](/documents/accessions-e.html) are as follows.     
They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/).

[WGS](/ddbj/wgs.html)

: BNHK01000001-BNHK01004035 (4,035 entries) [BNHK.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BN/BNHK.gz)

[DRA](/dra/index.html)

: DRR250115-DRR250120 (DRA010912)

Also, DDBJ released its related sequence data which is a part of sex chromosome.

[LC632297](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/getentry/na/LC632297/)

[DRA](/dra/index.html)

: DRR289452 (DRA011927)
