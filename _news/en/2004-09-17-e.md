<dd><a href="/services/past-services-e.html#gib">GIB (Genome Information Broker)</a> provides an integrated search of Bacteria, Archaea, Eukaryota complete genome sequences. Because the genome sequence data of <i>Bacillus cereus ZK</i> and <i>Bacillus licheniformis ATCC 14580</i> were released, we incorporated them to GIB, and now you can search those data.
