DDBJ released sequence data of crown-of-thorns starfish (*Acanthaster planci*), which had been submitted by Tokyo Institute of Technology.

[The accession numbers](/documents/accessions-e.html) are as follows.     
They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/).

[WGS](/ddbj/wgs.html)

: BLZE01000001-BLZE01003642 (3,642 entries) [BLZE.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BL/BLZE.gz)

: BLZC01000001-BLZC01003552 (3,552 entries) [BLZC.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BL/BLZC.gz)

[DRA](/dra/index.html)

: DRR235980-DRR235986 (DRA010432)

: DRR235997-DRR236002 (DRA010432)
