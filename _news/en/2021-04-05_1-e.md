DDBJ released sequence data of a species of endosymbiotic dinoflagellates (*Symbiodinium microadriaticum*), which had been submitted by Atmosphere and Ocean Research Institute, The University of Tokyo.

[The accession numbers](/documents/accessions-e.html) are as follows.     
They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/).

[WGS](/ddbj/wgs.html)
: BLBA01000001-BLBA01009829 (9,829 entries) [BLBA.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BL/BLBA.gz)

[DRA](/dra/index.html)
: DRR192154-DRR192160 (DRA008990)
