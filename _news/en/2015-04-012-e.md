<p>DDBJ released genome sequence data of tadpole shrimp (<em>Triops cancriformis</em>), which had been submitted by Keio University. </p>

<p><strong>The accession numbers</strong> are as follows. They are available on <a href="http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html" target="_blank">getentry</a> or <a href="http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/" target="_blank">DRASearch</a>. </p>

<p><strong><a href="/ddbj/wgs-e.html">WGS</a></strong>: BAYF01000001-BAYF01060629 (BAYF.gz) (60,629 entries) <br><strong>DRA run</strong>: DRR017999 </p>
