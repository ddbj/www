<p>DDBJ released genome sequence data of brown-marbled grouper (<span class="italic">Epinephelus fuscoguttatus</span>) and leopard coralgrouper (<span class="italic">Plectropomus leopardus</span>), which had been submitted by Sun Yat-sen University. </p>

<p><a href="/ddbj/documents/accessions.html">The accession numbers</a> are as follows. <br>They are available on <a href="http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html">getentry</a>. </p>

<dl>
    <dt><span class="italic">Epinephelus fuscoguttatus</span></dt>
    <dd>AP022675-AP022699 (25 entries) </dd>
    <dt><span class="italic">Plectropomus leopardus</span></dt>
    <dd>AP022700-AP022809 (110 entries) </dd>
</dl>
