Due to the [NIG supercomputer maintenance](https://sc.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/en/blog/2022-01-12-network/), submission, search and analysis services of Bioinformation and DDBJ Center will be unavailable during the following period.    

**[Schedule]**    
January 12 (Wed) 13:00 - 15:00 (JST)

**[Services]**    
- DDBJ Nucleotide Sequence Submission System (NSSS)
- DRA/GEA validation
- D-way
- GEA reviewer access
- NBDC Application System, JGA/AGD systems
- JGA search service
- DFAST
- getentry, ARSA search services
- DDBJ ftp Service
- NIG Supercomputer

We apologize for these inconveniences. We would appreciate your kind understanding and patience.
