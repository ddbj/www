        <li>DH462001-DH508173 (46,173 entries) GSS (fosmid end) <a href="https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/mass/Monosiga_ovata_GSS/Monosiga_ovata_GSS_071218_2.seq.gz">Monosiga_ovata_GSS_071218_2.seq.gz</a><br></li>
