We inform you of a long nonbusiness day from May 3th to May 6th (JST) (called as "Golden Week" in Japan).

After 7th of May (Tue, JST), we will be able to respond to requests or questions which are received during the period.

[DDBJ Nucleotide Sequence Submission System (NSSS)](/ddbj/web-submission-e.html) will be unavailable during the following period. 

May 2th (Thu), 2024 8:00 - May 7 (Tue), 2024 10:00 (JST)

Thank you in advance for your understanding and cooperation.
