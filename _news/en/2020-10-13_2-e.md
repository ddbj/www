## Release of sequence data of Japanese rhinoceros beetle (Trypoxylus dichotomus)

DDBJ released sequence data of Japanese rhinoceros beetle (<span
class="italic">Trypoxylus dichotomus</span>), which had been submitted
by Nihon BioData Corporation.

[The accession numbers](/documents/accessions.html) are as follows.  
They are available on
[getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or
[DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/).

[WGS](/ddbj/wgs-e.html)  
: BNES01000001-BNES01002347 (2,347 entries)
[BNES.gz](ftp://ftp.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/ddbj_database/wgs/BN/BNES.gz)

[DRA](/dra/index-e.html)  
: DRR243822 (DRA010769)
