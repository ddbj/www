<p>DDBJ released genome and transcriptome data of Japanese amberjack (<em>Seriola quinqueradiata</em>) which had been submitted by National Research Institute of Aquaculture. </p>

<p><a href="/documents/accessions.html">The accession numbers</a> are as follows. They are available on <a href="http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html">getentry</a> or <a href="http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/">DRASearch</a>.</p>

    <dt>Genome</dt>
    <dd><strong><a href="/ddbj/wgs-e.html">WGS</a></strong>: BDMU01000001-BDMU01000384 (384 entries) <a href="ftp://ftp.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/ddbj_database/wgs/BD/BDMU.gz" target="_blank">BDMU.gz</a></dd>
    <dt>Transcriptome</dt>
    <dd><strong><a href="/ddbj/tsa-e.html">TSA</a></strong>: IACH01000001-IACH01013125 (13,125 entries) <a href="ftp://ftp.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/ddbj_database/tsa/IACH.gz" target="_blank">IACH.gz</a></dd>
    <dd><strong><a href="/dra/index-e.html">DRA</a></strong>: DRR096276 (DRA005910)</dd>
</dl>
