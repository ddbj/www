<dd>In relation to the "new human gut metagenome WGS 353,805 entries" released by DDBJ on October 18, 2007, the thirteen files (BAAU.gz to BABG.gz), which were placed on /database/wgs/ or /database2/wgs/ directory<br> of <a href="ftp://ftp.ddbj.nig.ac.jp" target="_blank">anonymous FTP</a>,<br>had a problem that the each entry was duplicated.<br>The files contain flatfiles for human gut metagenome WGS data.
<dd>We deeply apologize for inconveniences.<br>The problem has been resolved now.<br>Please retrieve the corrected files again if you have got<br>defective files during the period until Nov. 12, 2007, 12:30 PM.<br>
