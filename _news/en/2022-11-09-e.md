* An accession number is automatically assigned to the submitted BioProject record.
* Updates to submitted BioProject records are reflected to the D-way BioProject OVERVIEW tabs.
* Enter an appropriate NCBI taxonomy name to the environmental BioProject.
* The [locus tag prefix](/ddbj/locus_tag-e.html) could be registered in both BioProject and BioSample. However, since 9th November 2022, the prefix can be registered only in BioSample.
