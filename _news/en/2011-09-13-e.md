    <dd> DDBJ newly released WGS and scaffold CON data derived from sake yeast (<em>Saccharomyces cerevisiae</em> Kyokai no. 7), which had been submitted by National Research Institute of Brewing.  (Available by <a href="http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html" target="_blank">getentry</a>) <strong>Reference:</strong> Sake yeast genome database (SYGD)<strong>The accession numbers</strong> are as follows;

        <ul>
            <li>WGS    BABQ01000001 - BABQ01000705 (BABQ.gz) ( 705 entries)</li>
            <li>scaffold CON    DG000037 - DG000052 ( 14 entries)</li>
            <li>mitochondrion    AP012028</li>
        </ul>
    </dd>
