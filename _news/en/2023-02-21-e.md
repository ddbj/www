[BioProject fields](/bioproject/project-info-e.html) were simplified.  
The following fields were removed.

* GENERAL INFO
	* Relevance
	* Consortium
* TARGET
	* Novel organism
	* Description
	* General Properties
	* Organism Replicons
	* Phenotype
	* Prokaryote Morphology
	* Ecological Environment
* PUBLICATION
	* Publication details for DOI
