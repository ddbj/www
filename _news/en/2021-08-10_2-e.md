DDBJ released sequence data of Japanese Chestnut (cultivar name Ginyose) (*Castanea crenata*), which had been submitted by Kazusa DNA Research Institute.

[The accession numbers](/documents/accessions-e.html) are as follows.     
They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/).

[WGS](/ddbj/wgs.html)

: BPMU01000001-BPMU01000781 (781 entries) [BPMU.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BP/BPMU.gz)

[DRA](/dra/index.html)

: DRR304281-DRR304284 (DRA012289)
