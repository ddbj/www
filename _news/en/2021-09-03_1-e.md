DDBJ released sequence data of mangrove cricket (*Apteronemobius asahinai*), which had been submitted by SOKENDAI.

[The accession numbers](/documents/accessions-e.html) are as follows.     
They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/).

[WGS](/ddbj/wgs.html)

: BPSV01000001-BPSV01151060 (151,060 entries) [BPSV.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BP/BPSV.gz)

[DRA](/dra/index.html)

: DRR300664-DRR300665 (DRA012220)
