DDBJ released sequence data of a species of sea slug (*Plakobranchus ocellatus*), which had been submitted by National Institute for Basic Biology.

[The accession numbers](/documents/accessions-e.html) are as follows.     
They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/).

[TSA](/ddbj/tsa.html)
: IABR01000001-IABR01112625 (112,625 entries) [IABR.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/tsa/IA/IABR.gz)

[DRA](/dra/index.html)
: DRR063265-DRR063267 (DRA004830)
