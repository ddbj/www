<p>Bioinformation and DDBJ Center will be closed for the New Year Holidays from December 28, 2019 (Sat) to January 5, 2020 (Sun).<br>Please note that <a href="/ddbj/web-submission-e.html">DDBJ Nucleotide Sequence Submission System (NSSS)</a> will be unavailable. Schedule is as follows.</p>

<p>BI-DDBJ office close: December 28, 2019 (Sat) - January 5, 2020 (Sun)<br>NSSS: December 26, 2019 (Thu) 18:00 - January 6, 2020 (Mon) 10:00 (JST)</p>

<p>NIG Supercomputer and DDBJ Web services (except <a href="/ddbj/web-submission-e.html">NSSS</a>) are available during the New Year Holidays. After January 6 (JST), we will respond to requests or questions which are received during this period.</p>

<p>Thank you in advance for your understanding and cooperation.</p>
