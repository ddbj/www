DDBJ released sequence data of a coreid bug (*Riptortus pedestris*), which had been submitted by Kyoto University.

The accession numbers are as follows.    
They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/).

[TSA](/ddbj/tsa-e.html)
: ICRD01000001-ICRD01143734 (143,734 entries) [ICRD.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/tsa/IC/ICRD.gz)

[DRA](/dra/index-e.html)
: DRR249470-DRR249479 (DRA010903)
