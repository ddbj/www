<dd><a href="/services/past-services-e.html#gib">GIB (Genome Information Broker for Microbial Genomes)</a> provides a comprehensive view of the complete microbial genome sequences. Twist</i> were released, we incorporated it to GIB, and now you can search those data.<br>
