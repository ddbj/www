<p>DDBJ, EMBL-Bank/EBI and GenBank/NCBI hold the INSDC meeting every year to maintain and update international collaboration for nucleotide sequence database construction.</p>

<p>In 2014, the meeting is held at DDBJ in Mishima, May 20-22.</p>

<p>DDBJ will report the decisions at the meeting on the DDBJ HP that are useful for the data submitters and users of DDBJ.</p>
