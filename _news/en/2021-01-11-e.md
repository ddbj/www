DDBJ released sequence data of a species of herbs (*Ophiorrhiza pumila*), which had been submitted by Kazusa DNA Research Institute.

[The accession numbers](/documents/accessions-e.html) are as follows.     
They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/).

[WGS](/ddbj/wgs-e.html)
: BLIW01000001-BLIW01000013 (13 entries) [BLIW.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BL/BLIW.gz)

[DRA](/dra/index-e.html)
: DRR194711-DRR194732 (DRA009076)
