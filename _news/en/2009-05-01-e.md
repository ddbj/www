DDBJ newly released 25,600 entries of EST data derived from Japanese common newt (Cynops pyrrhogaster), which had been submitted by Kyoto University.<br><br>The accession numbers are as follows;

<ul>
    <li>FS290155-FS315754 (25,600 entries)</li>
</ul>

<p>These entries were released as DDBJ daily updates on April 25, 2009, and available by <a href="http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html" target="_blank">getentry</a> or <a href="http://arsa.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html">ARSA</a>.</p>
