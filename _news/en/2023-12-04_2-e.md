Bioinformation and DDBJ Center will be closed for the New Year Holidays from December 29, 2023 (Fri) to January 3, 2024 (Wed).    
Please note that [DDBJ Nucleotide Sequence Submission System (NSSS)](/ddbj/web-submission-e.html) will also be unavailable on the following schedule. 

BI-DDBJ office close: December 29, 2023 (Fri) - January 3, 2024 (Wed)    
NSSS: December 27, 2023 (Wed) 10:00 - January 4, 2024 (Thu) 10:00 (JST)    

NIG Supercomputer and DDBJ Web services (except NSSS) are available during the New Year Holidays. After January 4 (JST), we will respond to requests or questions which are received during this period.    

MSS, BioProject, BioSample, DRA, GEA, JGA and MetaboBank system is available during the period, however, reviewing and responding to any inquiry will stop from December 29 to January 3 (JST).
