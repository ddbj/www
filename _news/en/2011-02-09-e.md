Whole genome sequencing data <a href="http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/submission?acc=DRA000307">DRA000307</a> of sake-brewing Japonica rice Omachi (Oryza sativa Japonica Group, cultivar Omachi) submitted by the <a href="http://www.nodai.ac.jp/english/">Tokyo Univetsity of Agriculture</a> have been released. SNP information is available at the <a href="http://www.nodai-genome.org/oryza_sativa_en.html">NGRC_Rice_Omachi Genome Browser</a> by the Tokyo Univetsity of Agriculture.
