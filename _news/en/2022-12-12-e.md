Bioinformation and DDBJ Center will be closed for the New Year Holidays from December 29, 2022 (Thr) to January 3, 2023 (Tue).    
Please note that [DDBJ Nucleotide Sequence Submission System (NSSS)](/ddbj/web-submission-e.html) and MetaboBank will also be unavailable on the following schedule. 

BI-DDBJ office close: December 29, 2022 (Thr) to January 3, 2023 (Tue) (JST)    
NSSS closing: December 23, 2022 (Fri) 9:00 - January 4, 2023 (Wed) 9:00 (JST)    
MetaboBank closing:  December 29, 2022 (Thr) to January 3, 2023 (Tue) (JST)

NIG Supercomputer and DDBJ Web services (except NSSS and Metabobank) are available during the New Year Holidays. After January 4 (JST), we will respond to requests or questions which are received during this period.    

BioProject, BioSample, DRA, GEA, and JGA system is available during the period, however, reviewing and responding to any inquiry will stop from December 29 to January 3 (JST).
