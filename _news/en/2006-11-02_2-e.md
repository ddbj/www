<dd>The honeybee genome sequencing consortium made up of 90 institutes reported the genome sequence of the honeybee, <i>Apis mellifera</i>on <a href="http://www.nature.com/nature/journal/v443/n7114/abs/nature05260.html" target="_blank">Nature 443, 931-949 (26 October 2006)</a>.<br>From Japan, <a href="http://genome.gsc.riken.jp/" target="_blank">RIKEN GSC</a> and others were associated with the consortium.<br>Honeybee EST entries submitted from RIKEN GSC were distributed from DDBJ (See <a href="#060731">previous report</a>).</dd>
