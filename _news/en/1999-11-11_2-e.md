<dd>Dec. 27, 1999 (Mon.) 9:00 Stop all the activities<br>
<dd>Jan. 5, 2000 (Wed.) Resume all the activities

    <p></p>
<dd>Thank you for your cooperation. We wish you a Happy New Year.
