DDBJ released sequence data of a species of bumblebee (*Bombus ignitus*), which had been submitted by National Agriculture and Food Research Organization.

[The accession numbers](/documents/accessions-e.html) are as follows.     
They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/).

[TSA](/ddbj/tsa.html)
: ICPS01000001-ICPS01132255 (132,255 entries) [ICPS.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/tsa/IC/ICPS.gz)

[DRA](/dra/index.html)
: DRR226102-DRR226111 (DRA010143)

[GEA]
: E-GEAD-356
