DDBJ released sequence data of a species of wrinkled frog (*Glandirana rugosa*), which had been submitted by Kyoto University.

[The accession numbers](/documents/accessions-e.html) are as follows.     
They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/).

[WGS](/ddbj/wgs.html)
: BLSH010000001-BLSH010561726 (561,726 entries) [BLSH.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BL/BLSH.gz)

[DRA](/dra/index.html)
: DRR221658-DRR221669 (DRA009996)
