DDBJ released sequence data of 
a diatom 
(*Chaetoceros tenuissimus*), 
which had been submitted by 
Japan Fisheries Research and Education Agency, National Research Institute of Fisheries Science.

[The accession numbers](/documents/accessions-e.html) are as follows.     
They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/).

Nuclear genome ([WGS](/ddbj/wgs-e.html))

: BLLK01000001-BLLK01000085 (85 entries) [BLLK.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BL/BLLK.gz)

Mitochondrial genome

: [LC537470](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/getentry/na/LC537470/)

Chloroplast genome

: [LC537471](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/getentry/na/LC537471/)

[DRA](/dra/index-e.html)

: DRR196215-DRR196219 (DRA009158)
