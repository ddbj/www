<p>DDBJ released transcriptome sequence data of welsh onion (<em>Allium fistulosum</em>), which had been submitted by Institute of Vegetables and Tea Science. </p>

<p><strong>The accession numbers</strong> are as follows. They are available on <a href="http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html" target="_blank">getentry</a> or <a href="http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/" target="_blank">DRASearch</a>. </p>

<p><strong><a href="/ddbj/tsa-e.html">TSA</a></strong>: FX553726-FX608587,FX657476-FX657516 (54,903 entries) <br><strong>DRA run</strong>: DRR006298-DRR006306 </p>
