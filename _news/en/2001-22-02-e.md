<p>We have been provided the web pages of DDBJ Center and annotated sequence database DDBJ at <a href="//www.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/index-e.html">www.ddbj.nig.ac.jp</a> domain, and the pages of the other BioProject/BioSample/DRA/JGA databases at <a href="//trace.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/index_e.html">trace.ddbj.nig.ac.jp</a> domain.<br>To improve usability of our websites, we will merge these two sites and release a new website. The <a href="//trace.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/index_e.html">trace.ddbj.nig.ac.jp</a> domain will be deprecated and will be merged into <a href="//www.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/index-e.html">www.ddbj.nig.ac.jp</a> domain.</p>

<p>The pages of each database will be as follows.</p>
<div class="sub_index">

    <ul>
        <li>DDBJ: www.ddbj.nig.ac.jp → www.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/ddbj/</li>
        <li>DRA: trace.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/dra → www.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/dra/</li>
        <li>BioProject: trace.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/bioproject → www.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/bioproject</li>
        <li>BioSample: trace.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/biosample → www.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/biosample</li>
        <li>JGA: trace.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/jga → www.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/jga</li>
    </ul>
</div>

<p>We will add new information by updating this news page.</p>

<p>We will redirect/rewrite old URLs to new ones as much as possible, but please remind that it is difficult to redirect all pages and files properly.</p>
