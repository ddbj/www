DDBJ released sequence data of 
kuruma prawn, *Penaeus japonicus*, 
which had been submitted by 
Tokyo University of Marine Science and Technology.

The accession numbers are as follows.    
They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/).

[TSA](/ddbj/tsa-e.html)
: ICRJ01000001-ICRJ01025415 (25,415 entries) [ICRJ.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/tsa/IC/ICRJ.gz)

[DRA](/dra/index-e.html)
: DRR274325-DRR274340 (DRA011525)

[TSA](/ddbj/tsa-e.html)
: ICRK01000001-ICRK01040991 (25,415 entries) [ICRK.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/tsa/IC/ICRK.gz)

[DRA](/dra/index-e.html)
: DRR274276-DRR274324 (DRA011525)
