DDBJ released sequence data of two-spotted cricket (*Gryllus bimaculatus*), which had been submitted by Harvard University.

[The accession numbers](/documents/accessions-e.html) are as follows.

They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/). 

[WGS](/ddbj/wgs-e.html)
: BOPP01000001-BOPP01047877 (47,877 entries) [BOPP.gz](ftp://ftp.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/ddbj_database/wgs/BO/BOPP.gz) 

[DRA](/dra/index-e.html)
: genome: DRR272308-DRR272314 (DRA011482-DRA011488)    
RNA-Seq: DRR255763-DRR255768 (DRA011117), DRR258387-DRR258410 (DRA011174)
