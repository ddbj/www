DDBJ released sequence data of a giant mealworm beetle (*Zophobas atratus*), which had been submitted by the Institute of Agrobiological Sciences, NARO.    
The accession numbers are as follows.

They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/).

TSA
: ICLG01000001-ICLG01091161 (91,161 entries) [ICLG.gz](ftp://ftp.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/ddbj_database/tsa/IC/ICLG.gz)

DRA
: DRR173275-DRR173278 (DRA008225)

GEA
: [E-GEAD-310](ftp://ftp.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/ddbj_database/gea/experiment/E-GEAD-000/E-GEAD-310/)
