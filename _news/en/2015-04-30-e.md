<p>DDBJ released transcriptome sequence data of Hokkaido salamander (<em>Hynobius retardatus</em>), which had been submitted by Hokkaido University. </p>

<p><strong>The accession numbers</strong> are as follows. They are available on <a href="http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html" target="_blank">getentry</a> or <a href="http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/" target="_blank">DRASearch</a>. </p>

<p><strong><a href="/ddbj/tsa-e.html">TSA</a></strong>: LE000001-LE740933 (740,933 entries) <br><strong>DRA Run</strong>: DRR016729-DRR016800 </p>
