DDBJ released sequence data of 
a species of free-living amoeba causing primary amebic meningoencephalitis 
(*Naegleria fowleri*), 
which had been submitted by 
Iwate University.

[The accession numbers](/documents/accessions-e.html) are as follows.     
They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/).

Nuclear genome ([WGS](/ddbj/wgs-e.html))

: BQFV01000001-BQFV01002493 (2,493 entries) [BQFV.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BQ/BQFV.gz)

Mitochondrial genome

: [AP025248](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/getentry/na/AP025248/)

[DRA](/dra/index-e.html)

: DRR326741 (DRA012968)
