<p class="top_space"><span class="red bold">*</span> <span class="bold">Termination of providing CDS fasta files:</span><br>We formerly provided fasta files for nucleotide sequences corresponding to CDS features in the 'cdsdb' directory under the 'ddbj' directory;<br>ftp.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/ddbj_database/ddbj/cdsdb/</p>

<p>However, the files have not been used so frequently and we have provided an alternative service via our web APIs, WABI;<br><a href="/services/wabi-e.html ">https://www.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/services/wabi-e.html </a></p>

<p>So, we decided to terminate the generation of CDS fasta files.<br>See also the following URL to use WABI;<br><a href="/services/getentry-e.html#ge_program">https://www.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/services/getentry-e.html#ge_program</a></p>
