<p><a href="http://www.insdc.org/">International Nucleotide Sequence Database Collaboration (INSDC)</a>, consisted of DDBJ Center, <a href="//www.ebi.ac.uk/">EBI</a> and <a href="//www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/">NCBI</a>, hold the international collaborators meeting every year.<br>In 2019, the meeting was held at EBI, 15-17 May, to discuss practical matters to maintain and update nucleotide sequence data archives; <a href="/index-e.html">DDBJ</a>, <a href="//www.ebi.ac.uk/ena/">ENA</a>, <a href="//www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/genbank/index.html">GenBank</a> and Sequence Read Archive (SRA).<br>The outcomes of the meeting are summarized <a href="/activities/index-e.html#2019">here</a>.</p>
