<html>The entries corresponding to complete sequences of japonica rice chromosomes<br>were updated from build 3 to build 4.<br>Their accession numbers are AP008207-AP008218.<br>The updated contents were the results annotated by The Second Rice Annotation<br>Project Meeting (RAP2) which was an international rice genome annotation project<br>organized by Japanese research groups.<br>By this updating, AP008207-AP008218 entries were redistributed with<br>approximately 28,000 CDS (protein coding region) features.

<p><b>The accession numbers</b>  (Anonymous FTP)  are as follows ; <br></p>

<ul>
    <li> AP008207-AP008218 (<a href="https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/mass/Oryza_sativa_Japonica_Group_CON/Oryza_sativa_Japonica_Group_CON_090807_1.seq.gz">Oryza_sativa_Japonica_Group_CON_090807_1.seq.gz</a>) (12 entries) </li>
</ul>
