<p>DDBJ released transcriptome sequence data of <em>Raphanus sativus</em> var. <em>sativus</em>, <em>Brassica rapa</em> subsp. <em>pekinensis</em> and their hybrid (<em>Brassica rapa</em> x <em>Raphanus sativus</em>), which had been submitted by Seoul National University. </p>

<p><strong>The accession numbers</strong> are as follows. They are available on <a href="http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html" target="_blank">getentry</a> or <a href="http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/" target="_blank">DRASearch</a>. </p>

<p><em>Raphanus sativus</em> var. <em>sativus</em><br>    <strong><a href="/ddbj/tsa-e.html">TSA</a></strong>: FX657517-FX691702 (34,186 entries) <br>    <strong>DRA run</strong>: DRR014232, DRR014233 </p>

<p><em>Brassica rapa</em> subsp. <em>pekinensis</em><br>    <strong><a href="/ddbj/tsa-e.html">TSA</a></strong>: FX691703-FX725989 (34,287 entries) <br>    <strong>DRA run</strong>: DRR014230, DRR014231 </p>

<p><em>Brassica rapa</em> x <em>Raphanus sativus</em><br>    <strong><a href="/ddbj/tsa-e.html">TSA</a></strong>: FX725990-FX799344 (73,355 entries) <br>    <strong>DRA run</strong>: DRR014228, DRR014229 </p>
