<p>DDBJ released genome and transcriptome sequence data of hops (<em>Humulus lupulus</em>), 2 varieties, which had been submitted by Suntory Global Innovation Center Limited. </p>

<p><strong>The accession numbers</strong> are as follows. They are available on <a href="http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html" target="_blank">getentry</a> or <a href="http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/" target="_blank">DRASearch</a>. </p>

<p></p>

<p><em>Humulus lupulus</em> var. <em>cordifolius</em> <br>    <strong><a href="/ddbj/con-e.html">scaffold CON</a></strong>: LD000001-LD132476 (132,476 entries) <br>    <strong><a href="/ddbj/wgs-e.html">WGS</a></strong>: BBPB01000001-BBPB01292698 (BBPB.gz) (292,698 entries) <br>    <strong>DRA run</strong>: DRR024452-DRR024456 </p>

<p><em>Humulus lupulus</em> var. <em>lupulus</em> <br>    <strong><a href="/ddbj/con-e.html">scaffold CON</a></strong>: LD132477-LD264952 (132,476 entries) <br>    <strong><a href="/ddbj/wgs-e.html">WGS</a></strong>: BBPC01000001-BBPC01292698 (BBPC.gz) (292,698 entries) <br>    <strong>DRA run</strong>: DRR024392-DRR024447 </p>

<p><em>Humulus lupulus</em> var. <em>lupulus</em> <br>    <strong><a href="/ddbj/tsa-e.html">TSA</a></strong>: LA328023-LA715951 (387,929 entries) <br>    <strong>DRA run</strong>: DRR024457-DRR024463 </p>
