DDBJ Center office will close for summer break.    
Please note that [DDBJ Nucleotide Sequence Submission System (NSSS)](/ddbj/web-submission-e.html) and 
NBDC Human Database Review System/JGA/AGD will be unavailable.  
Schedule is as follows.

BI-DDBJ office close: August 15 (Thu), 2024 - August 16 (Fri), 2024    
NSSS: August 9 (Fri), 2024 18:00 - August 19 (Mon), 2024 10:00 (JST)  
NBDC Human Database Review System/JGA/AGD: August 13 (Tue), 2024 - August 16 (Fri), 2024  

After August 19 (Mon), we will respond to requests or questions which are received during the period of the nonbusiness day.    
Our response will be delayed than usual before and after the above period.

Thank you in advance for your understanding and cooperation.
