DDBJ released sequence data of four species belonging to Flaveria genus (*Flaveria bidentis*, *Flaveria brownii*, *Flaveria floridana*, *Flaveria robusta*), which had been submitted by Kwansei Gakuin University.

[The accession numbers](/documents/accessions-e.html) are as follows.     
They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/).

[WGS](/ddbj/wgs.html)
: BJCM01000001-BJCM01276228 (276,228 entries) [BJCM.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BJ/BJCM.gz)

[DRA](/dra/index.html)
