<p>DDBJ released genome sequence data of eggplant (<em>Solanum melongena</em>), which had been submitted by Kazusa DNA Research Institute. </p>

<p><strong>Reference</strong>; <br><a href="http://eggplant.kazusa.or.jp" target="_new">Eggplant Genome DataBase</a> </p>

<p><strong>The accession numbers</strong> are as follows. They are available on <a href="http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html" target="_blank">getentry</a> or <a href="http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/" target="_blank">DRASearch</a>. </p>

<p>    <strong><a href="/ddbj/con-e.html">scaffold CON</a></strong>: DF357214-DF384212 (26,999 entries) <br>    <strong><a href="/ddbj/wgs-e.html">WGS</a></strong>: BAUE01000001-BAUE01143048 (BAUE.gz) (143,048 entries)<br>    <strong><a href="/dra/index-e.html">SRA</a></strong>: DRR014074-DRR014076 </p>
