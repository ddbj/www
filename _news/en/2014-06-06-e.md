<p>DDBJ released genome sequence data of radish (<em>Raphanus sativus</em>), 8 cultivars, which had been submitted by Kazusa DNA Research Institute. </p>

<p><strong>Reference</strong>;<br>Database: <a href="http://radish.kazusa.or.jp/" target="_blank">Raphanus sativus Genome DataBase</a><br>Academic paper: <a href="http://dnaresearch.oxfordjournals.org/content/early/2014/05/13/dnares.dsu014.full%20target=" _blank>Draft Sequences of the Radish (<em>Raphanus sativus</em> L.) Genome</a> </p>

<p><strong>The accession numbers</strong> are as follows. They are available on <a href="http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html" target="_blank">getentry</a> or <a href="http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/" target="_blank">DRASearch</a>. </p>

<p><em>Raphanus sativus</em> cv. Aokubi S-h <br>    <strong><a href="/ddbj/con-e.html">scaffold CON</a></strong>: DF384214-DF396802 (12,589 entries) <br>    <strong><a href="/ddbj/wgs-e.html">WGS</a></strong>: BAUK01000001-BAUK01101710 (BAUK.gz) (101,710 entries) <br>    <strong><a href="/ddbj/gss-e.html">GSS</a></strong>: GA872392-GA901611 (29,220 entries) </p>
