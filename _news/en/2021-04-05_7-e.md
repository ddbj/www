DDBJ released sequence data of a species of vase tunicate (*Ciona intestinalis*), which had been submitted by Kyoto University.

[The accession numbers](/documents/accessions-e.html) are as follows.     
They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/).

[WGS](/ddbj/wgs.html)
: BNJZ01000001-BNJZ01000518 (518 entries) [BNJZ.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BN/BNJZ.gz)

[DRA](/dra/index.html)
: DRR253162-DRR253167, DRR253172-DRR253179 (DRA011024)

[WGS](/ddbj/wgs.html)
: BNKA01000001-BNKA01000306 (306 entries) [BNKA.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BN/BNKA.gz)

[DRA](/dra/index.html)
: DRR253169-DRR253171 (DRA011024)
