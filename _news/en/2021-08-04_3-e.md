DDBJ released sequence data of 6 species of Drosophila genus flies (*Drosophila nasuta*, *Drosophila albomicans*, *Drosophila americana*, *Drosophila americana texana*, *Drosophila kohkoa*, *Drosophila novamexicana*), which had been submitted by Tokyo Metropolitan University.

[The accession numbers](/documents/accessions-e.html) are as follows.     
They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/).

[WGS](/ddbj/wgs.html)

*Drosophila nasuta*

: BJEH01000001-BJEH01000604 (604 entries) [BJEH.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BJ/BJEH.gz)

*Drosophila albomicans*

: BJEI01000001-BJEI01000834 (834 entries) [BJEI.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BJ/BJEI.gz)

*Drosophila americana*

: BJEJ01000001-BJEJ01000435 (435 entries) [BJEJ.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BJ/BJEJ.gz)

*Drosophila americana texana*

: BJEK01000001-BJEK01000199 (199 entries) [BJEK.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BJ/BJEK.gz)

*Drosophila kohkoa*

: BJEL01000001-BJEL01000087 (87 entries) [BJEL.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BJ/BJEL.gz)

*Drosophila novamexicana*

: BJEM01000001-BJEM01000270 (270 entries) [BJEM.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BJ/BJEM.gz)
