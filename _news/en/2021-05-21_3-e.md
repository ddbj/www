DDBJ released sequence data of a species of strawberry (*Fragaria x ananassa*), which had been submitted by Kazusa DNA Research Institute.

[The accession numbers](/documents/accessions-e.html) are as follows.     
They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/).

[WGS](/ddbj/wgs.html)
: BOVN01000001-BOVN01032771 (32,771 entries) [BOVN.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BO/BOVN.gz)

[DRA](/dra/index.html)
: DRR160118-DRR160175 (DRA007606, DRA007607)
