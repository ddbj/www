DDBJ released sequence data of a species of stick insect (*Entoria okinawaensis*), which had been submitted by Database Center for Life Science.

[The accession numbers](/documents/accessions-e.html) are as follows.     
They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/).

[TSA](/ddbj/tsa.html)
: IADO01000001-IADO01201677 (201,677 entries) [IADO.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/tsa/IA/IADO.gz)

[DRA](/dra/index.html)
: DRR148118-DRR148120 (DRA007226)

[GEA]
: E-GEAD-295
