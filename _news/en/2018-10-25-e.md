<p>Nucleic Acids Research paper reporting DDBJ Center's activities was published.</p>

<div id="pub-list">

    <ul>
        <li>
            <span class="title"><a href="https://academic.oup.com/nar/advance-article/doi/10.1093/nar/gky1002/5144146">DDBJ update: the Genomic Expression Archive (GEA) for functional genomics data</a></span>
            <span class="authors">Kodama Y, Mashima J, Kosuge T, Ogasawara O</span>
            <span class="pub-info">
                <span class="journal">Nucleic Acids Res</span>
                <span class="bib-info">24 October 2018</span>
                <span class="pubmed-id">doi: 10.1093/nar/gky1002</span>
            </span> <!-- .pub-info -->
        </li>
    </ul>

</div>

<p>The major contents are as follows.</p>

<div class="sub_index">

    <ul class="disc">
        <li>Updates of our public databases, web services and NIG supercomputer.</li>
        <li>Since July 2018, the new database <a href="https://www.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/gea/index.html">"Genomic Expression Archive (GEA)"</a> has been accepting functional genomics data submissions such as gene expression, epigenetics and SNP microarray. The public GEA data will be indexed by <a href="https://www.ebi.ac.uk/arrayexpress/">EBI ArrayExpress</a>.</li>
        <li>BioSample <a href="https://www.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/biosample/validation-e.html">validator</a> and submission processing automation.</li>
        <li><a href="https://sc.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/ja/application/individual-genome-analysis-system">NIG Supercomputer for personal genome analysis (Japanese text only)</a> started in September 2018.</li>
    </ul>
</div>
