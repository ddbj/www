Due to the update of the account registration system, the name of the submitter account will change from "D-way account" to "DDBJ account." For more information, see [here](https://www.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/ddbj-account-e.html).

[Added on 2025-03-25]    
When you login for the first time with both new and/or existing account, an email will be sent to confirm your email address. Follow the instructions in the email.
