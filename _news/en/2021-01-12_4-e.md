DDBJ released sequence data of blunt-leaved bogmoss (*Sphagnum palustre*), which had been submitted by Kumamoto University.

[The accession numbers](/acc_def-e.html) are as follows.    
They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/)

[TSA](/ddbj/tsa-e.html)
: ICRE01000001-ICRE01191610 (191,610 entries) [ICRE.gz](ftp://ftp.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/ddbj_database/tsa/IC/ICRE.gz)

[DRA](/dra/index-e.html)
: DRR197119-DRR197122 (DRA009217)
