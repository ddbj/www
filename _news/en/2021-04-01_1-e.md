DDBJ released sequence data of a species of narrow-leaved vetch (*Vicia sativa*), which had been submitted by Kazusa DNA Research Institute.

[The accession numbers](/documents/accessions-e.html) are as follows.     
They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/).

[WGS](/ddbj/wgs.html)
: BLWO01000001-BLWO01054083 (54,083 entries) [BLWO.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BL/BLWO.gz)

[DRA](/dra/index.html)
: DRR053677-DRR053689 (DRA004347)
