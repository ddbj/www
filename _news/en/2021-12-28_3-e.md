DDBJ released sequence data of 
orb-weaver spiders, 
*Caerostris darwini* (Darwin's bark spider), 
*Caerostris extrusa* 
which had been submitted by 
Keio University.

[The accession numbers](/documents/accessions-e.html) are as follows.     
They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/).

[WGS](/ddbj/wgs-e.html)

*Caerostris darwini*

: BPLQ01000001-BPLQ01015733 (15,733 entries) [BPLQ.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BP/BPLQ.gz)

*Caerostris extrusa*

: BPLR01000001-BPLR01021729 (21,729 entries) [BPLR.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BP/BPLR.gz)

[DRA](/dra/index-e.html)

*Caerostris darwini*

: DRR287754, DRR287755 (DRA011880)

*Caerostris extrusa*

: DRR287718 (DRA011873)
