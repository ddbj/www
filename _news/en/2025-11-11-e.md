Entries publicized from GenBank during the following period have not been reflected in getentry and DDBJ ftp.

The affected entries are as follows:
- WGS and TPA-WGS entries from GenBank distributed on and after September 3, 2025

Reason
- DDBJ system has repeatedly failed to download the WGS and TPA-WGS data from GenBank since September, 2025.

We sincerely apologize for any inconvenience this may cause. We are now inquiring the tool so that it can correctly retrieve the data from GenBank.
