DDBJ released sequence data of 
two cultivars of cherry tree 
(*Prunus kanzakura*), 
Atami-zakura and Kawazu-zakura, 
which had been submitted by 
Kazusa DNA Research Institute.

[The accession numbers](/documents/accessions-e.html) are as follows.     
They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/).

[WGS](/ddbj/wgs.html)

Atami-zakura

: BPUL01000001-BPUL01002180 (2,180 entries) [BPUL.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BP/BPUL.gz)

Kawazu-zakura

: BPUM01000001-BPUM01001544 (1,544 entries) [BPUM.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BP/BPUM.gz)

[DRA](/dra/index.html)

Atami-zakura

: DRR313277-DRR313278 (DRA012553)

Kawazu-zakura

: DRR313280-DRR313281 (DRA012553)
