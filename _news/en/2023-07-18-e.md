DDBJ Center office will close for summer break.    
Please note that [DDBJ Nucleotide Sequence Submission System (NSSS)](/ddbj/web-submission-e.html) will be unavailable. Schedule is as follows.

BI-DDBJ office close: August 11 (Fri), 2023 - August 16 (Wed), 2023    
NSSS: August 10 (Thu), 2023 10:00 - August 17 (Thu), 2023 10:00 (JST)

After August 17 (Thu), we will respond to requests or questions which are received during the period of the nonbusiness day.    
Our response will be delayed than usual before and after the above period.

Thank you in advance for your understanding and cooperation.
