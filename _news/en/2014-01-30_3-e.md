<p><strong>Reference</strong>；<a href="http://www.oist.jp/news-center/news/2014/1/14/world%E2%80%99s-first-simultaneous-genetic-sequencing-host-coral-and-symbiont" target="_new">OIST NEWS CENTER</a></p>
