DDBJ released sequence data of cowpea (*Vigna unguiculata subsp. dekindtiana*), which had been submitted by National Agriculture and Food Research Organization.

[The accession numbers](/documents/accessions-e.html) are as follows.     
They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/).

[WGS](/ddbj/wgs.html)
: BLZF01000001-BLZF01004285 (4,285 entries) [BLZF.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BL/BLZF.gz)

[DRA](/dra/index.html)
: DRR180434 (DRA008484)
