<p>We at DDBJ Center will temporarily close for the New Year Holidays from December 29 (Sat), 2018 to January 3 (Thu), 2019.<br>Please note that <a href="/ddbj/web-submission-e.html">DDBJ Nucleotide Sequence Submission System</a> will be unavailable. Schedule is as follows.<br>NIG Supercomputer and DDBJ network services (except <a href="/ddbj/web-submission-e.html">DDBJ Nucleotide Sequence Submission System</a>) <br>are available during the New Year Holidays.</p>

<p>During this period, DDBJ will continue to receive inquiries from E-mail and "Contact us" web form.<br>But, please note that the reply from DDBJ will be after the reopening of the office on January 4, 2019.</p>
