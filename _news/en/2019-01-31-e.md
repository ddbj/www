<p>Dear DDBJ Pipeline users, </p>

<p>DDBJ Pipeline, NGS data analysis service, will be closed February 2019. </p>

<ul>
    <li>Login to the DDBJ Pipeline will be impossible after 15 February, so, please download the necessary data before closing.</li>
    <li>Uploaded data (query file and reference genome file) will be deleted after the end of service. </li>
    <li>We introduce Maser as alternative analysis service, a successor of DDBJ Pipeline.<br>Since user accounts and data are not taken over, please registrate from the following site.<br><a href="http://cell-innovation.nig.ac.jp/public/contents/service.html">http://cell-innovation.nig.ac.jp/public/contents/service.html</a></li>
</ul>

<p class="top_space">National Institute of Genetics<br>DDBJ Pipeline development team</p>
