<dd>GIB (Genome Information Broker) provides an integrated search of Bacteria, Archaea, Eukaryota complete genome sequences.
<dd>Because the genome sequence data of <i>Granulobacter bethesdensis CGDNIH1 </i> (by NIH), <i>Nitrosomonas eutropha C71</i>, <i>Shewanella frigidimarina NCIMB 400</i>, <i>Syntrophomonas wolfei subsp. wolfei str. Goettingen</i> and <i>Maricaulis maris MCS10</i> (by JGI) were released, we incorporated them to GIB, and now you can search those data.</dd>
