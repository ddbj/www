* getentry
* ARSA
* DDBJ Nucleotide Sequence Submission System
* D-way
* GEA reviewer access
* DRA sftp service
* JGA/AGD sftp service
* DDBJ FTP service

**[Schedule (JST)]**    
July 24th (Mon) 10:00 - July 26th (Wed) 15:00 (JST)    

**[Affected services]**    
* DRA validation
* GEA validation
