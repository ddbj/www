<dd>GIB (Genome Information Broker) provides an integrated search of Bacteria, Archaea, Eukaryota complete genome sequences. Because the genome sequence data of <i>Thermococcus kodakaraensis KOD1</i> and <i>Staphylococcus aureus subsp. aureus COL</i> were released by Kyoto Univ. and TIGR, we incorporated them to GIB, and now you can search those data.
