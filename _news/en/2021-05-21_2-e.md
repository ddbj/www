DDBJ released sequence data of two species of sea slug (*Plakobranchus ocellatus*, *Elysia marginata*), which had been submitted by National Institute for Basic Biology.

[The accession numbers](/documents/accessions-e.html) are as follows.     
They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/).

*Plakobranchus ocellatus*
[WGS](/ddbj/wgs.html)
: BLXT01000001-BLXT01008647 (8,647 entries) [BLXT.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BL/BLXT.gz)

[DRA](/dra/index.html)
: DRR029525-DRR029530 (DRA003001)

*Elysia marginata*
[WGS](/ddbj/wgs.html)
: BMAT01000001-BMAT01014149 (14,149 entries) [BMAT.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BM/BMAT.gz)

[DRA](/dra/index.html)
: DRR238951-DRR238956 (DRA010577)
