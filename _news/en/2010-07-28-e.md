    <dd>DDBJ newly released GSS: 113,551 entries and EST: 101,079 entries derived from fission yeast (<em>Schizosaccharomyces pombe</em> ), which had been submitted by Osaka City University.<strong>The accession numbers</strong>  (Anonymous FTP)  are as follows ;

        <ul>
            <li>GSS: FT321169-FT434719 (<a href="https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/mass/Schizosaccharomyces_pombe_GSS/">Schizosaccharomyces_pombe_GSS_100728_1.seq.gz</a>) (113,551 entries)</li>
            <li>EST: FY072959-FY174037 (<a href="https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/mass/Schizosaccharomyces_pombe_EST/">Schizosaccharomyces_pombe_EST_100728_1.seq.gz</a>) (101,079 entries)</li>
        </ul>These entries were released as DDBJ daily updates on Jul. 28, 2010.(Photo: Integrated Database Project)
    </dd>
