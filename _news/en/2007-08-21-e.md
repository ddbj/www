<dd>Total number of CON entris greatly increased from 983,699 to 3,932,751 in the latest DDBJ release (DDBJ Rel.70).<br>This big growth is caused by the addition of 4,087,206 entries, submitted from <a href="http://www.jcvi.org/">J. Craig Venter Institute</a>, in 2007 April released so far.
<dd>The data are the results of marine metagenomic research, and details are introduced in the following papers.
