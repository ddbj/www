<dd>GIB (Genome Information Broker) provides an integrated search of Bacteria, Archaea, Eukaryota complete genome sequences. Because the genome sequence data of <i>Campylobacter jejuni RM1221</i> and <i>Dehalococcoides ethenogenes 195</i> were released by TIGR, we incorporated them to GIB, and now you can search those data.

<dd><small><b><i>Dehalococcoides ethenogenes 195</i>:</b> Genome Sequence of the PCE-Dechlorinating Bacterium, Dehalococcoides ethenogenes, Science 307 (5706), 105-108 (2005)</small></dd>
