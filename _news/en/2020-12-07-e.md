<p>Bioinformation and DDBJ Center will be closed for the New Year Holidays from December 29, 2020 (Tue) to January 3, 2021 (Sun).<br>Please note that <a href="/ddbj/websub-e.html">DDBJ Nucleotide Sequence Submission System (NSSS)</a> will be unavailable. Schedule is as follows.</p><p>BI-DDBJ office close: December 29, 2020 (Tue) - January 3, 2021 (Sun)<br>NSSS: December 25, 2020 (Fri) 10:00 - January 4, 2021 (Mon) 10:00 (JST)</p><p>NIG Supercomputer and DDBJ Web services (except <a href="/ddbj/websub-e.html">NSSS</a>) are available during the New Year Holidays. After January 4 (JST), we will respond to requests or questions which are received during this period.</p>
