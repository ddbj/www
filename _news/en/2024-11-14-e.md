<p>A paper regarding INSDC was published in <a href="https://academic.oup.com/nar/advance-article/doi/10.1093/nar/gkae1058/7899524">Nucleic Acids Research Database Issue</a>. This paper reports recent INSDC activities including expanding the collaboration to be more representative of the global community.</p>
<p>From Bioinformation and DDBJ Center, Masanori Arita (Head) and Yasukazu Nakamura (Head of International Affairs Division) contributed as authors.</p>
