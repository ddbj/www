DDBJ released sequence data of 4 species of Golden Orb Weaver (*Trichonephila clavata*, *Trichonephila clavipes*, *Trichonephila inaurata madagascariensis*, *Nephila pilipes*), which had been submitted by Keio University.

[The accession numbers](/documents/accessions-e.html) are as follows.     
They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/).

[WGS](/ddbj/wgs.html)

*Trichonephila clavata*

: BMAO01000001-BMAO01039792 (39,792 entries) [BMAO.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BM/BMAO.gz)

*Trichonephila clavipes*

: BMAU01000001-BMAU01021438 (21,438 entries) [BMAU.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BM/BMAU.gz)

*Trichonephila inaurata madagascariensis*

: BMAV01000001-BMAV01028263 (28,263 entries) [BMAV.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BM/BMAV.gz)

*Nephila pilipes*

: BMAW01000001-BMAW01132898 (132,898 entries) [BMAW.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BM/BMAW.gz)


[DRA](/dra/index.html)

*Trichonephila clavata*

: DRR232341-DRR232343 (DRA010308)

*Trichonephila clavipes*

: DRR235161-DRR235164 (DRA010409)

*Trichonephila inaurata madagascariensis*

: DRR235165-DRR235166 (DRA010410)

*Nephila pilipes*

: DRR240645-DRR240646 (DRA010658)
