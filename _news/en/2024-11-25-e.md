Due to the NIG supercomputer maintenance during the National Institute of Genetics’s planned blackout, submission, search and analysis services of DDBJ Center will be unavailable as below.    
 During this period, we will not be able to issue accession numbers nor distribute data.

**December 13th (Fri) 13:00 - December 20th (Fri) 12:00**

- DDBJ Nucleotide Sequence Submission System (NSSS) (December <span class="red">12th (Thu) 8:00</span> - December 20th (Fri) 12:00)
- MSS form
- D-way Submission system
  - BioProject
  - BioSample
  - DRA
  - GEA
  - GEA reviewer access
  - DRA/GEA/MSS/MetaboBank SFTP (ftp-private.ddbj.nig.ac.jp) services
  - Because of the D-way account system downtime, the following services will be unavailable.
    - New account creation
    - Account information update
    - Password change
- DRA/GEA validation (December <span class="red">12th (Thu) 14:00</span> - December 20th (Fri) 12:00)
- JGA/AGD
- Metabobank
- NBDC Human Database Application System
- NBDC Group Sharing Database Application System
- DFAST
- DDBJ FTP Service (December 13th (Fri) <span class="red">17:00</span> - December <span class="red">19th(Thu) 24:00</span>)
- getentry, ARSA, DDBJ Search search services
- DDBJ Group Cloud Service
- NIG Supercomputer (December 13th (Fri) <span class="red">17:00</span> - December <span class="red">19th (Thu) 24:00</span>)


Thank you for your understanding and cooperation.
