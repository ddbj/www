Since 13:40 JST on September 5, 2025, all DDBJ systems and the supercomputer have been out of service due to a power outage. All services have now been restored. We sincerely apologize for the inconvenience caused to our users.

[Supercomputer Shutdown Due to Power Outage at NIG on September 8, 2025](https://sc.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/en/blog/2025-09-08-blackout/)

----

<span class="red">All services have been restored.</span>
