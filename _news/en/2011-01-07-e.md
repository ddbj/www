dra directory composition of <a href="/services/index-e.html ">DDBJ FTP </a>site was changed.The data which had been placed under /ddbj_database/dra are available in "fastq", and SRA lite formatted data are newly available in "sralite". SRA lite contains sequence and fastq data.For details, please see <a href="https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/README.TXT">README.TXT</a>. If you automatically monitor DDBJ anonymous FTP, please confirm your monitoring program if necessary.
