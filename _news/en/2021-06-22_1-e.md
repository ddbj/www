DDBJ released sequence data of a species of tardigrade (*Paramacrobiotus sp. TYO*), which had been submitted by the University of Tokyo.

[The accession numbers](/documents/accessions-e.html) are as follows.     
They are available on [getentry](http://getentry.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html) or [DRASearch](http://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/DRASearch/).

[WGS](/ddbj/wgs.html)
: BHEN01000001-BHEN01000684 (684 entries) [BHEN.gz](https://ddbj.nig.ac.jp/public/ddbj_database/wgs/BH/BHEN.gz)

[DRA](/dra/index.html)
: DRR144969, DRR144970 (DRA007104)
: DRR146886 (DRA007144)
