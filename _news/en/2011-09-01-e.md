A new directory "bioproject" was added under "<a href="ftp://ftp.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/ddbj_database/">ddbj_database</a>" of DDBJ anonymous FTP site. All of released data from<a href="/bioproject/index-e.html"> DDBJ BioProject</a> will be available at the new "bioproject" directory.About the "ddbj_database" directory and its sub-directories, please refer to the <a href="ftp://ftp.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/ddbj_database/README.TXT">README.TXT</a> in the directory. If you monitor the DDBJ anonymous FTP, please confirm your monitoring program if necessary.
