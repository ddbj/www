If you want to change email addresses of Contact and Other Person before the Accession number is issued, please send a request email to mass@ddbj.nig.ac.jp with the title included your NSUB number. After the Accession number has been issued, please contact us from "Update Contact Person Information" on our [site](https://www.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/ddbj/update.html).
